Definition of Premature Ejaculation

A common definition of premature ejaculation is the inability to delay ejaculation until it is mutually desirable by both partners.  This is very subjective, and can vary from 5-10 minutes to hours depending on whom you ask.  How long a man is able to delay orgasm is not really the issue when defining and diagnosing premature ejaculation.  The real question is whether both partners are satisfied by the sexual encounter.
