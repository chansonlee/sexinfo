Positions that Help with Premature Ejaculation

Because muscle tension in the legs and pelvic region leads to greater chances of orgasm, any position that required less muscle tone would help with premature ejaculation.  The woman-on-top (or other-partner-on-top) position is very useful for many reasons besides allowing the man to relax and focus on the feelings he has learned to identify as his sexual response.  The side-by-side position is also useful.
