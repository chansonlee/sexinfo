Environmental and Biological Causes of Premature Ejaculation

Masturbation is a taboo subject in western society.  While many people and groups are showing more acceptance, masturbation is still a very touchy subject for many people.  Because boys are much more likely to masturbate at an early age, an age when they are likely to be living with other people (parents, siblings, etc) and not have much privacy, hurried masturbation, being punished for masturbation, and anxiety about masturbation are likely to occur.  After learning a sexual response in the situation where ‘faster is better’ is it any wonder why premature ejaculation is so common?
