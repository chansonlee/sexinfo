Submission Assigner States for Writers

ASSIGNMENTS OPEN
1) Fresh submission, unassigned
	- All options available
2) Assigned to someone else
	- Remove "Assigned"
	- Remove "Unassigned"
3) Assigned to self
	- All options available

	- Remove "Assigned"
	- Remove "Unassigned"
2) Assigned to someone else
	- Remove "Assigned"
	- Remove "Unassigned"
3) Assigned to self
	- Remove "Assigned"
	- Remove "Unassigned"
